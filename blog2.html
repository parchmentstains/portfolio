Missing Ramadan

  • Go back to Home

Despite all the morning caffeines and ghee-porota, I’m badly missing Ramadan. I’m missing the alluring scent of piyaju and beguni coming from the extremely adjacent neighbouring kitchen, while their sizzle keeps on being an equal delight to my ears.

I’m missing that holy essence of midnight which magically entrapped my restrictive mind to attend to two more rakat of prayers. I’m missing the idyllic feeling of sitting on the prayer mat during dawn and listening to the zikr of the universe. Ramadan is a month that comes with gifts in form of feelings that cannot be fathomed, unless felt.

While I might not be able to conjure all those feelings as Ramadan 2021 is a past now, I can surely hope for Ramadan 2022 to come and sit sooner and cherish the memories I’ve made, till then!
