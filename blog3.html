The book that I’ve recently finished reading contains this beautiful quote by Blaise Pascal: “I love poverty because Jesus loved it. I love wealth because it affords me the means of helping the needy.” My instant reaction after reading this quotation was: Isn’t it something that I’ve always felt deep inside but never been able to articulate this properly and accurately?!

When I was a kid, I used to wonder a lot on the ever existing socio economic differences between people and why Almighty chose to award some with more money than others. As the question was never asked to any elders, I never received any matured answer. But back in those days, I was very much curious about the Sunnah lifestyle. I guess it was some children’s book on Prophet Pbuh where I was finally able to get some answer to my question.

Since then, I’ve tried to disburse no matter how much little the amount is and have tried to make sure that it goes to the ones who’re actually in need of it. I believe Almighty considers me worthy of being a fair distributor and I make every attempt at being so.

While charity totally depends on person to person, I think if we all consider ourselves Almighty’s honoured agents and act accordingly, the world can surly be made a bit more beautiful.
